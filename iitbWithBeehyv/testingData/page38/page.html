    Product Description
    In the fast world of today, where we spend over nine hours either at a desk or on a sofa, health is one of the last things that we think of. So, get the Apple Watch Series 2 that comes with a wide range of features which will help you keep a track of your fitness goals. With the Apple Watch Series 2, managing your health is now going to be much easier.
    Built-In GPS

    With a built-in GPS feature that records your distance, speed and pace, the Apple Watch Series 2 helps in monitoring outdoor activities like walking, running and cycling more effectively. Thanks to this features, this smartwatch displays the map of the route you took on your iPhone. It also shows you where you ran the fastest.This way, you can build your stamina and speed in an organized manner.

    Heart Rate Sensor

    With the Apple workout app, you can also keep a track of your heart rate. Yes, the watch measures your heart rate too. Just ensure that the watch fits you snugly for an accurate reading.

    Splash, Surf and Swim

    The Apple Watch Series 2 is water-resistant to a depth of 50 m so you don't need to have second thoughts before jumping into a pool. Also, the speakers of the watch have been reinvented to let water in and then force it back with the help of sound vibrations.

    An Effective Workout App

    The watch has an effective workout app that accurately assesses your movements so you know how effectively you're working out, whether you are swimming, biking, running or jogging on a treadmill. The watch lets you select up to five metrics to make workout more fun.

    The Inspiration To Keep Moving

    With the Apple smartwatch on your wrist, you are more likely to take an active interest in physical activities. The watch has activity rings that let you know if you have been sitting too much inspiring you to take more steps and burn more calories if you're being too inactive.. This way, you can ensure that you get 30 minutes of exercise each day, even if it’s not all at once. The watch also comes with smart notifications that give you regular updates on your progress for the motivation you need.

    Share Activity Rings

    Share seemingly impossible fitness goals with friends so you can step it up in the competitive spirit with them. Compete with each other to see who reaches the goal first. Doesn’t this make your goals more fun to achieve? The watch also sends you distinctive badges that you can share each time you reach an achievement.

    Activity Metrics For Wheelchair Users

    No, Apple does not leave the differently abled out in designing a watch for fitness lovers. It also features two wheelchair-workouts to help wheelchair users maintain their fitness levels.

    For Mindfulness And Relaxation

    While not all of us have the time to practice yoga or for an other relaxation activity, the least we can do is keep a track on our breathing everyday. The watch’s Breath App guides you through this. The app helps you with a series of deep breaths and gives you reminders to work on your breathing everyday.

    Third Party Help Apps

    You can also install a range of third party apps, based on your lifestyle, to fulfill your fitness goals. These apps help you stay active by reminding you of your workout regimens and routines. While Beddit gives you a summary of your sleep cycles, Pocket Yoga guides you through yoga sessions and WaterMinder lets you know when it’s time for another glass of water.

    The Health App On Your iPhone

    Once you've completed your workout routine for the day you can view all the information from the various health and fitness apps you have installed on the dashboard overview for a clearer understanding of your fitness trends.

    A Great Performance

    A dual-core processor and a powerful GPU ensure that your favourite apps launch instantly and you can receive updated information at all times. With smooth animations and quick app performance, apps are now fun to use. The watch also has a bright screen that gives you a clear display.

    Easy Notifications

    The watch alerts you each time you get a message. With a tap you can read messages and respond in a variety of fun and expressive ways. You can also view who is calling on your wrist and choose whether to answer or dismiss the call. With invitations, you'll never miss an event staying punctual always.

    The Face: Beauty Meets Utility

    You can instantly launch any app with a tap and move between different faces with a swipe. You can pick information on your watch face. In this way, you can keep track of a lot at a glance. You can access the dock through the side button and navigate through apps with ease.

    Siri To Help You

    By saying "hey siri" you can perform a variety of tasks like checking messages and emails, searching for songs or finding out if you have closed your move ring.
