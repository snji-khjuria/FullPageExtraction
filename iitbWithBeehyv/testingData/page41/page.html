    The Micromax Canvas Knight 2 is your source of entertainment and style. Carry this Android smartphone with you wherever you go and kick boredom out in style.

    Watch online shows, download files, make Internet calls and much more in a jiffy with the Micromax Canvas Knight 2, thanks to its 4G connectivity.
