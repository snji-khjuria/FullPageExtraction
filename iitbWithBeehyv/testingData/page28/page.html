    Description

    The Flipkart SmartBuy induction cooktop is designed for faster and safer cooking. Energy-efficient and compact, this elegant cooktop is perfect for your kitchen or office pantry.

    Read More
    Product Description
    Faster Cooking

    Thanks to its large heating element, the 1800W Flipkart SmartBuy induction cooktop heats food 85-percent faster than regular gas stoves. As a result, it is highly efficient and aids in speeding up the cooking process.

    Energy Efficient

    Up to 50-percent more energy-efficient than conventional gas burners, the Flipkart SmartBuy Induction cooktop consumes less energy. The wattage is consumed as per size of the vessel and its thickness. Not only that, you can check the power consumption for a particular duration post cooking.

    LED Indicators for Easy Operation

    The appliance features an LED display that helps you operate it with ease. The cooktop comes with a digital timer control that lets you control and set the cooking temperature as per the dish.

    Pre-set Menus

    This user-friendly induction cooktop comes with 7 preset menus. These pre-set cooking functions include Pressure Cook, Saute, Curry, Heat Milk, Dosa/Chapati, Deep Fry and Idli modes.

    Automatic Shut Off Feature

    This safety feature automatically turns the heating element off in the event of overheating. It also brings the temperature down if you remove the pan or if the hotplate has been left on for an extended period.

    Overheat Protection

    This induction cooktop switches off automatically if the pot or pan gets too hot. It will start again after a few minutes when the pot cools down.

    Pan Detection

    If a pan’s material or its dimensions are not suitable for this induction cooktop, the “Heating Indicator Light” won't turn on. In addition, the induction cooker will shut off automatically after 60 seconds.

    Elegant and Compact Design

    Featuring a compact design, this induction cooktop is perfect all types of kitchens. Further adding to your kitchen's decor is the neat and glossy surface of this cooktop.
