    Product Description
    Don’t like carrying your bulky DSLR camera everywhere you go, but love capturing amazing photos and selfies? Then this sleek and stylish Mi A1 is what you need. With this smartphone’s dual cameras, you can capture life’s best moments without compromising on the clarity and color.
    12 MP Wide-angle and 12 MP Telephoto Lenses

    The dual lenses work together to give you a real, SLR-like bokeh effect.

    Zoom Lenses

    Distance won’t be an issue to capture far away shots as its telephoto lens enables up to 2x optical zoom, and it’s paired with up to 10x digital zoom.

    Beautify 3.0

    This smart filter detects hundreds of facial points and automatically accentuates the portraits.

    Google Photos

    Store unlimited memories and get easy access to shared albums on this Android One smartphone.

    Snapdragon 625 and 4 GB RAM

    Ensures a glitch-free multitasking experience so you can use multiple apps simultaneously without facing lag.

    13.97 cm Full HD Display

    The 2.5D curved glass display, along with the Corning Gorilla Glass, makes for a beautiful display and ensures durability.

    7.3 mm Thin

    Features a full body metal design that is easy to hold, fingerprint resistant and stylish to look at.

    High-quality Audio

    This smartphone combines a dedicated amplifier and an advanced Dirac HD Sound algorithm to provide you with high-fidelity audio along with boosted speaker volume and deeper lows.

    Other Features

    The wide-angle lens features a 26 mm equivalent focal length and a two-tone flash while the telephoto lens features a 50 mm equivalent focal length. Features a 3080 mAh battery and comes with 64 GB internal storage space.
