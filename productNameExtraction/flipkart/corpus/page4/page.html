    Product Description
    Packed with features, such as a dual rear camera system, the Android Nougat 7.1.1 OS, a 4000 mAh battery, 3 GB of RAM and a sleek metal body, the Lenovo K8 Plus is built to perform.
    Dual Rear Camera System

    Take picture-perfect photos with the dual rear camera system (13 MP + 5 MP cameras). In addition to the Purecell Plus sensor and the depth sensor, it is equipped with the Imagiq V2 0.8-5.6 focal ratio technology and Largan precision lenses for bright and clear photos.

    Say Cheese to the Front Camera

    The 8 MP front camera comes with a party flash and the Pro Mode for beautiful selfies even in low light conditions.

    The Android Nougat 7.1.1 Experience

    Switch between apps by double tapping. Also, reply on the go without opening any app by having mini conversations within your notification. Use Data Saver to make the data pack last longer. Make use of the multi-window display option to view two windows simultaneously.

    A Battery You Can Bank Upon

    The 4000 mAh battery can keep the fun going on for up to 48 hours. It offers up to 20 hours of video watching and up to 13 hours of internet browsing. With the rapid charging feature, you get hours of battery power in just a few minutes.

    Sleek Yet Tough Body

    The metal body features 2.5D glass and curved edges, adding to your convenience. The screen comes with Corning Gorilla Glass 3 for improved protection.

    Full-on Entertainment

    Enjoy an immersive audiovisual experience, thanks to the Dolby Atmos and TheaterMax technologies. Watch every detail in high definition on the 13.20 cm (5.2) FHD display. Use the Music Key to take complete control of the multimedia on your phone. It can also be programmed as a shortcut to launch any app of your choice.

    Fingerprint Reader

    Unlock your device, access private data, make mobile payments with participating retailers - do all that and more with the multi-function fingerprint reader.

    Storage and Connectivity

    Add up to an additional 128 GB of internal memory to the existing 32 GB by using a microSD card. It comes with three slots in one tray for two SIM cards and one microSD card.

    Awe-inspiring Performance

    Experience a smooth and fast performance with the 2.5 GHz MTK P25 octa-core processor and 3 GB of RAM.
