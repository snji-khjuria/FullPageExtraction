    • Product Description
    Why rely on an expensive repairman, when you can gift your husband this Bosch 108-piece tool kit and watch him enjoy making DIY furniture or repairing creaking kitchen cabinet doors. This multi-functional tool kit is designed to make your life easier and better.
    Why You Need this Multifunctional Tool Box?

    With 108 different pieces that can be used for various repairs around the house, this toolbox is easily a one-stop solution for all your home repairing needs and more. The tools are designed to ensure a professional level of work as well as being easy to use.

    Ratchet Handle

    Made from quality TPR material, the ratchet handle is designed for a smooth, quick and easy use. This one handle supports multiple usages and has a dual direction whirl design that makes it easy for you to screw and unscrew items.

    Screw Bits

    The handle can be fitted with either precision bits, sockets or screw bits according to the need. The precision bits are designed for fixing items such as toys, clocks, mobile phones and other devices. The chromium-vanadium steel sockets are mainly used for assembling and disassembling objects that come with hex head cap screw. For all your furniture assembly, toys and electrical appliances repairs, you can use the durable screw bits.

    Claw Hammer

    Whether you are hanging a photo frame or setting up a furniture unit, this sturdy and easy-to-handle claw hammer can come in handy. It features a sharp claw that can be used to easily remove nails, while the strong head can be used to hammer in the nails or set things properly. The hammer has a thermoplastic elastomers soft gum handle that offers a comfortable and secure grip while you are working.

    Pliers

    The long nose plier and the pincer plier can be used to grip small objects, hold wires, bend loops and attach wires. Both these pliers feature good quality heads and are made from chromium-vanadium steel and come with ergonomic grip.

    Adjustable Wrench

    Be it fixing a leaky tap or tightening your childu0027s bicycleu0027s seat post, this adjustable wrench can come in handy. Itu0027s 15cm in length, easy to use and the head comes with precision worm technology screw that adjusts smoothly.

    Hand Saw

    You can work on your DIY wood-based projects with ease and precision using this hand saw. It features an ergonomic handle for a comfortable grip. The saw blade is tough and sharp and can easily cut through wood. The saw is designed to be used for thin wood-based projects only.

    Measuring Tape

    DIY projects require precise measurements and to help you measure various dimensions of the objects easily you can use this measuring tape that has three lock check buttons. It is safety-certified by CE (European Standards).

    Flashlight

    Find what you are looking for in your dimly-lit storeroom using this 9-pearls design LED flashlight. A handy compact torch that easily fits the palm of your hand, this flashlight comes energy-saving features for a long lasting use.

    For Drilling

    From hanging paintings on the wall to setting up a wall stand, you can use the drilling tools in this toolkit to make your work easier. It comes with masonry drills, plastic bolts, and screws.

    Utility Knife

    You can use this utility knife to cut thin plastic sheets, insulation tape and sandpaper easily. For safety, it comes with an automatic locking mechanism.

    L-Shaped Hex Key

    The set of L-shaped hex keys is useful for fixing things such as bicycles, motorcycles and DIY furniture that come with hexagonal sockets. You can use these hex shaped keys to tighten or loosen bolts and screws that have a hexagonal socket in the head. These keys are made from quality chromium-vanadium steel and are strong and durable.

    Electrical Tape

    Ensure safety from electrical shocks by insulating damaged wires and machine parts with this electrical tape. It is a CSA-certified product and can withstand heat and current, making it safe to use on any electrical devices.

    Voltage Detector Tester Pen

    After you have repaired an electrical appliance such as a blender, you can check if itu0027s working by using this pen. It features a light that glows when thereu0027s a live circuit.
