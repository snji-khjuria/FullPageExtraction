    • Description

    Forty years ago, a family member of the powerful Vanger plan mysteriously disappears from the island inhibited by them. Her uncle now appoints a journalist and a computer hacker to solve the mystery. The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo is about how to people unintentionally dig up the dark secrets of a powerful family.

    Summary of the Book

    The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo will certainly keep you on your toes and make sure that you wont be able to put it down until you flip the last page. Harriet Vanger disappeared from a family gathering held on an island forty years ago. Her uncle believes that she was murdered by someone belonging the the Vanger clan.

    Mikael Blomkvist a disgraced financial journalist and Lisbeth Salander a tattooed computer hacker are called upon by Harriets uncle to solve the case. As they start investigating one thing leads to another until Blomkvist and Salander find themselves in a situation wherein protecting themselves becomes more important.

    About Stieg Larrson

    Stieg Larrson was a Swedish author and journalist. His genre of interest was crime. Larrson penned down books like The Girl Who Played with Fire and The Air Castle That was Blown Up.
