    Description

    Whip up chutneys, grind masalas and prepare yummy shakes effortlessly with the Flipkart Smartbuy Classico 500W Mixer Grinder. This mixer grinder, with its multipurpose jars, makes cooking convenient, less time consuming and fun. It also comes with a powerful 500W overload protected motor which is designed to offer a durable performance.

    Read More
    Product Description
    Powerful Performance

    This mixer grinder is equipped with a heavy duty 500W hybrid motor, which is overload protected, and it is built for a long-lasting performance. As a result, you can blend and grind, both, dry and wet ingredients with ease.

    Stainless Steel Jars

    The Flipkart Smartbuy Classico mixer grinder comes with a 1L liquidizing jar, a 0.7L grinding jar and a 0.3L chutney jar. Whether you have to blend yogurt and fruits smoothies, grind masalas or delicious chutneys, the Classico with its stainless-steel multipurpose jars is up to any task.

    Stainless Steel Blades

    The durable, stainless-steel jars are equipped with sharp stainless-steel blades to cut through almost any food item with ease, even coconut and whole, dry spices. Their sharpness remains the same even after regular use.

    3-Speed Control Knob

    This mixer grinder is equipped with a 3-speed control knob which allows you to get the ingredients blended, grounded and emulsified just as fine or coarse as you want them to be. All you need to do is set the Classico’s speed knob according to your needs and let it do all the hard work.

    Anti-spill Plastic Lids with Rubber Ring

    The Classico’s jars come with anti-spill plastic lids, so you can blend your favourite smoothies and make chutneys without worrying about those messy spills.

    Anti-slip Vacuum Feet

    Classico is equipped with specially designed vacuum feet that ensure a firm, sturdy grip on your kitchen counter.

    Overload Protection

    The overload protection feature protects the motor from any damage when the jars are overloaded or when the grinder has been overused.
