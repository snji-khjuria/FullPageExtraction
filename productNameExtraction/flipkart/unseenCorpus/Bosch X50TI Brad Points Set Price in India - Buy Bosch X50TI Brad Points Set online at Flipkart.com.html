    • Description

    Calling for a professional help for every little drilling job at home is a thing of past. Equip your house with this all-in-one set of brad points from Bosch, and fix it on your own.

    Drill Bits

    The metal drill bits are coated with titanium nitride to pave the way for faster and efficient drilling, making them durable. This pack also contains masonry drill bits with carbide tips to make them heat-resistant and enhance precision. The sharp wood drill bits come with centered tips and shoulder cutters to drill a hole precisely. The spade bits also come with titanium nitride coating for an improved drilling experience.

    Pack of 50

    This versatile pack contains 11 metal drill bits of diameters ranging from 1.5 mm to 6.5 mm, 6 masonry drill bits of diameters ranging from 4 mm to10 mm, 5 wood drill bits of diameters ranging from 4 mm to 10 mm, 2 spade bits of diameters 16/22 mm, 18 screwdriver bits with diameters ranging from 1mm to 25 mm, 3 nutsetters of diameter 7/8/10 mm, 1 diameter gauge, 1 knife, 1 socket adapter for nutsetters, 1 magnetic universal holder, and 1 countersink.
