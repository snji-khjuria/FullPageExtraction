    Product Description
    Stylish, portable and efficient - the JBL Tune Bluetooth speaker has almost everything to address the needs of an on-the-go music lover.
    Design

    With a compact and lightweight body, this wireless speaker is portable enough to be easily carried in your bag. Not only that, it is also features a stylish design, further adding to its appeal.

    Wireless Bluetooth Streaming

    Compatible with a host of Bluetooth-enabled devices, such as smartphones and tablets, this speaker allows you to stream your favourite tracks wirelessly through Bluetooth.

    Connectivity

    Apart from the Bluetooth connectivity, this speaker also lets you connect your mobile devices to it via a micro USB cable. You can also directly insert your SD or TF card into the slot allotted for them to play music on this JBL speaker. The 3.5 mm auxiliary port lets you effortlessly connect your headphones to it.

    Compatibility

    This portable speaker from JBL can support an array of formats, such as MP3, WMA and WAV.

    Rechargeable Li-ion Battery

    The inbuilt Li-ion battery of this speaker can be easily recharged. Once fully charged, it can play music for up to 6 hours at a stretch.

    Built-in FM

    Thanks to the built-in FM radio module, you can now enjoy music from across popular FM channels. Just tune in to your favourite radio station and listen to the latest musical hits.

    Built-in Microphone

    Featuring an inbuilt microphone, this Bluetooth speaker can double up as a conference phone system.

    LED Display Screen

    The built-in LED display lets you keep a track of your batterys charge. It also comes in handy when you want to tune in to your favorite radio channel.
