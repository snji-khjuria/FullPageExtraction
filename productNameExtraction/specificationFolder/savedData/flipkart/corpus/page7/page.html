    Product Description
    Boasting impressive features, such the Android Nougat operating system, a long-lasting battery, excellent cameras and a powerful processor, the Moto C Plus packs quite a punch. With its sleek profile and stunning display, this smartphone is engineered to impress.
    Android Nougat

    The Moto C Plus is powered by the Android Nougat operating system. It lets you easily switch between apps by double tapping. In addition, you can also run two apps side by side simultaneously. Reply on the go without opening any app by having mini conversations within your notification. This operating system also lets you rearrange your Quick Setting icons. As a result, you can access your most-used apps faster than ever.

    Cameras

    Love pub-hopping with your buddies on a Saturday night? Capture the fun times with the 2 MP front camera of this smartphone. Thanks to its selfie light, all your photos will look bright and clear, even the ones taken inside a dimly lit pub. The Moto C Plus also comes with an 8 MP rear camera. It features an LED flash to help you capture flaunt-worthy photos in any lighting condition.

    Long-lasting Battery

    With the Moto C Plus, theres never a dull moment. It comes equipped with a 4000 mAh battery that just wont call it a day, even if you do. Watch movies, play games, text friends or talk to your loved ones - the powerful battery can go on for up to 30 hours on a single full charge. As a result, you wont have to keep looking for power outlets.

    Powerful Performance

    Run resource-hungry applications on your phone, browse the Internet for information, play online games - do all this and more. With the Moto C Plus, the fun has just begun. It boasts a 1.3 Ghz 64-bit quad core processor and 2 GB of RAM, making multitasking a breeze. To top that off, the Moto C Plus features 4G support for a seamless internet browsing experience.

    Multiple Card Slots

    The Moto C Plus comes with two dedicated SIM slots, so you can keep your personal life separate from your professional life. In addition, it also features a dedicated slot for an SD card, so you can expand the 16 GB of internal memory to make room for more photos, songs and videos.

    Stunning Design and Display

    The Moto C Plus's back cover features a micro-textured finish, making it look sleek and elegant. Whether youre watching a movie on Netflix, shopping online for a dress, reading an e-book or playing mobile games, even the smallest of details will come to life on the vibrant 12.7 cm (5) HD display of this smartphone.
