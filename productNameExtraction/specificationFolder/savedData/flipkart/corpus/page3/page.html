    Product Description
    A+ Grade Panel

    With an A+ grade IPS panel on this Vu TV, you never have to worry about a bad viewing experience. Say goodbye to errant lines of light or other display errors messing up your favourite movie, TV show or that match you’ve been waiting for.

    Enjoy from Every Angle

    This TV comes with an Achromatic panel that offers a wider viewing angle. So it doesn’t matter where you are sitting, you’re going to have a good time watching TV. This is definitely a must when you have your friends over for a movie marathon or a binge-watch session.

    Quality Audio

    With the jumbo-sized box-type design speakers, high-quality audio is ensured for you. This powerful speakers offer more stereo separation than traditional TVs. So if you’re watching a concert or a match, it’s going to feel like you’re really there at the venue. On top of this, you get the company’s Digital Noise Reduction (DNR) system for crystal-clear audio.

    Master the Connectivity Game

    The cool thing about this Vu TV is that you aren’t restricted to watching only that what your cable provider offers. Bring all your local content onto the big screen with pen drives, external HDDs, HDMI- and VGA-enabled devices and enjoy a variety of content from the comfort of your couch. Now, no device gets left out!

    Put Your Headphones on

    You do not have to worry about disturbing your family or friends when you want to watch something late at night. This Vu TV lets you connect a pair of headphones to it, so you’ll be able to enjoy watching TV without worrying about waking up the whole house, or the neighbourhood, if you like your audio really cranked up.

    Other Features

    The pair of speakers of this TV offer an audio output of 7 + 7 W. The 98 cm (39) Full HD display of this TV offers 1920x1080p resolution. It also comes with two HDMI ports and two USB ports for maximum connectivity.
