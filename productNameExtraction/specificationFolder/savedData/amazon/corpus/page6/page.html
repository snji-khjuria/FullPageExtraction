Product description

Product description

This Sukkhi Incredible Gold Plated AD Necklace Set For Women is made of Alloy. Women love jewellery; specially traditional jewellery adore a women. They wear it on different occasion. They have special importance on ring ceremony, wedding and festive time. They can also wear it on regular basics. Make your moment memorable with this range. This jewel set features a unique one of a kind traditional embellish with antic finish.

From the Manufacturer






Outstanding Design

Layered design is what gives the piece of necklace an out-of-the-world look. The necklace boasts different types of silhouette that bring out the exquisiteness of the jewellery. It features intricate designs of a scroll, geometric detail, floral and intertwined curves that are decorated by loads of Austrian diamonds, giving a shimmering dimension to the necklace. A royal teardrop centre with a green stone and a round green stone-studded top give perfect contrast to the neckpiece. The maang tikka and the pair of earrings boast an elegant teardrop design with royal coloured stones to match the necklace. Each piece in this Sukkhi necklace set features pearl-like teardrop danglers for a classy look.







Make

This Austrian diamond necklace set is made using a solid metal that is gold-plated to give that perfect ethnic look.







Style Tips

This Sukkhi Incredible Austrian diamond necklace set will look best on your engagement or wedding outfit.
