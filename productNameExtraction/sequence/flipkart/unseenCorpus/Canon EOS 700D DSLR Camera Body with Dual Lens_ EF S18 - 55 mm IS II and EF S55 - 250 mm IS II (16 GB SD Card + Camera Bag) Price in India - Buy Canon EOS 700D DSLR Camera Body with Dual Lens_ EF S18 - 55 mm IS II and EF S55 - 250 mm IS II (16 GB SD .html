    Product Description
    The Canon EOS 700D offers a solid basic performance for all entry-level DSLR photographers. It delivers high quality images, has various functions and allows you to take high resolution movies.
    9-Point Auto Focus System

    This DSLR camera is equipped with an AutoFocus feature that ensures quick, accurate continuous shooting. While taking shots using the Viewfinder, the EOS 700D has an improved autofocus with a 9-point all cross-type AF system for accurate focus.
