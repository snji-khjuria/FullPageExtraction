    Product Description
    Would you rather wash clothes manually, or sit back, drink a cup of coffee and relax after a tiring day at work? If you prefer the latter, then get this fully automatic washing machine from Mitashi. Boasting a host of innovative features, this 6 kg washing machine is designed to take the hard work out of washing clothes.
    Powerful Spin Speeds

    This front-load washing machine from Mitashi offers spin speeds of up to 1000 RPM for maximum spinning of your clothes. By doing so it ensures that your clothes need less time for drying once they are taken out from the washing machine.

    Eight Wash Programs

    This washing machine comes with eight automatic wash programs, such as Rinse + Spin, Spin only, Cotton, Tub Clean, Delicate, Mix Wash, Synthetic and Quick 15. They not only make the machine easy to operate, but you can also select a program according to the kind of fabric you are washing and the wash load.

    Rust-free Body

    This washing machine boasts a fibre construction, making its body rust-free and durable.

    Universal Motor

    Equipped with the Universal Inverter Motor technology, this front-load washing machine keeps noise to a minimum and is energy-efficient, offering you an optimum washing performance.

    Noise- and Shock-proof Design

    Featuring a noise-proof design, this washing machine ensures a smooth washing performance without making too much noise. Since it is shock-proof, you can safely touch the machines body even when its in use.

    Lint Filter

    This fully automatic washing machine has a lint filter that protects the machines drainage pipe from any kind of blockage.

    Overload Protector

    If the washer is overloaded, the overload protector detects it and automatically stops the machine, thus protecting the motor from any potential damage.

    Self-balance System

    If you forgot to set the right wash program, dont worry. Thanks to its Self-balance system, this washing machine re-adjusts the wash program according to your wash load. If it detects excess load, the machine automatically sets the correct wash program.

    Self-cleaning Drum

    With this washing machines self-cleaning drum, you dont need to exert yourself anymore. Once you are done washing clothes, you can set the machine to self-clean itself. This cycle will not only remove the debris thats settled on the drum after each wash, but it will also improve the overall performance of the washing machine.

    Quick Wash

    The Quick 15 wash program of this washing machine makes it convenient for you to wash small loads of lightly soiled clothes in about 15 minutes. When you forget to put dampened clothes out in the sun, they might end up smelling. The Quick 15 program can also come in handy during those times as it helps in restoring the freshness of lightly dampened clothes.

    Heating Wash Technology

    You clothes might look clean from the outside, but are you sure they are thoroughly clean? Since this machine utilizes the Heating Wash technology, it ensures that your clothes are properly washed. This innovative feature takes advantage of water thats as hot as 96-degree Celsius to remove tough stains and allergens from your clothes. It also ensures that detergent is fully diluted in the washing water to provide a clean, quick and energy-efficient washing experience.

    Child Lock

    Kids are curious by nature. Which is why, it is important to be careful with appliances, lest they press a switch that they arent supposed to. This washing machine has a child lock feature that prevents your naughty little one from pressing any button that might change the washing cycle or interfere with other programs.

    Buzzer

    This fully automatic washing machine comes equipped with a buzzer that indicates the end of a wash cycle. Thats one less thing to worry about for you.
