    Product Description
    Assassin's Creed : Rogue is the next installment of the extremely popular video game series, Assassin's Creed, that takes you through perilous seas and corrupt lands. The story revolves around Shay Patrick Cormac, one of the assassins in the Order, who turns rogue when a mission for the Brotherhood goes wrong.
    Become the Ultimate Assassin

    In addition to being a highly skilled Master Assassin, Shay also has a number of amazing skills and weapons that can be used to your advantage. Equip your deadly air rifle for long and short range combat and protect yourself against other Assassins with your enhanced eagle vision.

    Descend Into Darkness

    As the game progresses, you will witness the transformation of Shay's character from an adventurous Assassin to a committed Templar who hunts his former brothers. Experience first-hand the events that leads Shay down this dark path which could change the fate of the Brotherhood forever.

    Improved Naval Gameplay

    Cast your ship, the Morrigan, into the high seas in an all-new improved naval gameplay. Fight your way through the icy seas of the North Atlantic by defending your ship from other crews using burning oil and other enemy tactics. You can even use the massive Icebergs as cover during Naval battles.

    New Environments

    In this game, you will explore new environments such as the cold winds of the North Atlantic Ocean, the hybrid setting of the River Valley and a fully recreated 18th Century New York City.
