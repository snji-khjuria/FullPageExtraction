    Product Description
    Bring home this Mitashi 6.5 kg semi-automatic top-load washing machine, which gives you superior cleaning with its Boomerage pulsator. Get lint-free clothes as this washing machines comes with a lint filter.
    Boomerage Pulsator

    The Boomerage Pulsator with its to-and-fro circular motion ensures that detergent enriched water penetrates your clothes to deliver superior cleaning.

    Two Wash Programs

    Easily operate the washing machine with the two wash programs: gentle wash and power wash.

    Rust-free and Non-corrosive Body

    The washing machines fiber body keeps rust at bay, making it non-corrosive.

    Buzzer

    This feature indicates the end of a wash cycle.

    Lint Filter

    This filter protects the pipe from getting clogged by eliminating lint formed during the wash.

    Overload Protection for Motors

    A small circuit automatically detects machine overload, protecting the motor from damage.

    High Castor Wheels

    These wheels help to move the washing machine around with ease.
