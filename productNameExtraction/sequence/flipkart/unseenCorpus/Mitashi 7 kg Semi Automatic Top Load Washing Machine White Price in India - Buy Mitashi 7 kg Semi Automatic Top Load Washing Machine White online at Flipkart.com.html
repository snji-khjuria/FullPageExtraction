    Product Description
    Bring home this Mitashi 7 kg semi-automatic top-load washing machine, which will simplify washing clothes, and save you valuable time. While the power scrubber and the soak function give you the perfect wash, the air jet dryer dries your clothes quickly.
    Air Jet Dryer Technology

    This technology allows fresh air to circulate inside the spinning tub to help the clothes dry faster, saving time and electricity. This also keeps the tub from getting moldy.

    Two Wash Programs

    Easily operate the washing machine with the two wash programs: gentle wash and power wash.

    Power Scrubber

    The built-in scrubber adds friction to the wash cycle to give your clothes a perfect wash.

    Overload Protection for Motors

    A small circuit automatically detects machine overload, protecting the motor from damage.

    Rust-free and Non-corrosive Body

    The washing machine’s fiber body keeps rust at bay, making it non-corrosive.

    Lint Filter

    This filter protects the pipe from getting clogged by eliminating lint formed during the wash.

    Soak Function

    Often used as a pre-wash cycle, this function shuts off the machine to soak the clothes in water and detergent before the wash cycle.

    High Castor Wheels

    These wheels help to move the washing machine around with ease.

    Buzzer

    This feature indicates the end of a wash cycle.
