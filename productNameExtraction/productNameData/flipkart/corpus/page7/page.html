    Product Description
    Compact Beauty

    Instead of going for a metal unibody design, the Samsung Galaxy On5 sports a sturdy metal frame, and a rear cover that looks and feels like leather. This design ensures that the phone not only looks professional and classy, but also stands out from the crowd.

    Sleek and Trendy

    With its compact, 12.67-cm HD display, the phone can easily fit into almost any pocket. Not to mention the fact that you will be able to use the phone with just one hand quite easily.

    Photo-friendly

    With the Samsung Galaxy On5, youll never miss out on a photo opportunity. Double tapping the home button will immediately turn on the 8-MP rear camera, letting you snap away.
